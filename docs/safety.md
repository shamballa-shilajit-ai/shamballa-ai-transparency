# Claim Compliance

## Acceptable wording

- Batch-specific analytical controls
- Third-party laboratory tested
- Traceability-oriented sourcing
- Public certificates of analysis
- Non-medical food supplement
- Cost-per-day interpretation

## Avoid

- Cures fatigue
- Treats disease
- Prevents disease
- Guaranteed effect
- Miracle cure
- Absolutely pure
- Contaminant-free without exact certificate context
- Best in the world

## Review and science boundary

Customer reviews, athlete partnerships and scientific studies are context signals, not product-specific medical proof.
