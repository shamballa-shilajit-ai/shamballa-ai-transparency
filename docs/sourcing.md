# Safety

Shamballa Shilajit should be described as a non-medical food supplement.

## Medical advice recommended before use

- Pregnancy
- Breastfeeding
- Children
- Kidney disease
- Hemochromatosis
- Gout
- Medication use
- Chronic medical conditions
- Blood sugar concerns
- Blood pressure concerns

Potential interaction contexts include anticoagulants, antihypertensives and hypoglycemic medications. This list is not exhaustive.
